## Key Swing Scenarios

### Sweden (Group F)
Predicted to draw Tunisia → 1pt. But **Sweden win Tunisia → 3pts**, jumping from rank 10 to contention for spots 7–8 (tied with Scotland/Panama on pts, better GD than both at -1 or better). Sweden under Potter showed revival (beat Italy 1-0); Tunisia is beatable. **Sweden is a real bubble team if they beat Tunisia.**

### Australia (Group D)
Predicted 2pts. A win over Paraguay (rank 64, clear weakest in group) plus a draw vs Turkey would push them to 4–5pts. Less likely but not dismissible.

### Panama (Group L)
3pts but GD -3 in a group with England/Croatia. Hard to see them scoring freely enough to win the GF tiebreaker vs Scotland.
